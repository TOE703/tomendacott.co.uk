About Chris

After a 3 year round the world motorbike journey covering 4 continents, 34 countries, 55,000 miles and 2 broken bones, I went to Merton college to study motorcycle mechanics.
Following brief periods of employment at Merton and SOS recovery I joined a dealership in 2006. After 10 years gaining a wealth of expertise I brached out on my own.
I am passionate about all elements of motorcycle engineering, in particular the challenges presented by electrical systems.
My interest in adventure travel continued and in 2015 I organised the a charity ride down to Porto, Portugal on old 2-Strokes.
I also love windsurfing and jazz - preferably at the same time!
