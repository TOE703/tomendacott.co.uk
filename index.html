About Tom

After leaving school and being led by my passion of motorbikes I ended up working in the motorcycle industry for nearly ten years. The feeling of needing a change and thirst for adventure grew stronger so I took a late 'gap year' and spent six months in South East Asia experiencing the wealth of culture. Meeting incredibly interesting people, both local and also travelling, I found myself gravitating towards web design and development. Completing a full years college course in only six months got me on the road to my new career.
